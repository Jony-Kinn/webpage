About Me

I am Seithati Mohashole, a student at Limkokwing University of Creative Technology, pursuing a Bachelor of Arts in Broadcasting and Journalism. I have a strong passion for graphic design, which I have honed through various school projects. My experience includes designing magazine spreads, creating eye-catching magazine cover pages, and developing portfolios that showcase creativity and professionalism.

What inspires me is the ability to communicate ideas visually and tell compelling stories through design. I'm dedicated to combining my skills in broadcasting and journalism with my design expertise to create impactful and engaging content.
